This website, designed and developed by Ishani Koul, brings together three works in the field of design: Mismatch: How inclusion shaped design by Kat Holmes, Design for the Real World by Victor Papanek, and Wrote the Book on User-Friendly Design. What I See Today Horrifies Me by Don Norman.

Each piece explores critical themes in design—ranging from inclusivity and sustainability to the ethics of user-centered design. Crafted with accessibility, simplicity, and clarity at its core, the site embodies the values these authors advocate, offering a thoughtful and engaging platform for their timeless insights.
